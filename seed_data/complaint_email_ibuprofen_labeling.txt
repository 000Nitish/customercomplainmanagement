Complaint Description:
During routine shelf audit at our distribution center, we identified a critical labeling discrepancy on blister packs from batch FDF-IBU-2024-0441. The printed expiry date reads "EXP 06/2026" when the batch record and our receiving documentation indicate the correct expiry should be "EXP 06/2025".

Approximately 12,000 blister packs may be affected. Product has NOT been dispensed to patients at our retail locations, but stock has been quarantined pending your response.

This appears to be a packaging/labeling defect with potential patient safety implications if product with incorrect expiry reaches market. We believe this may require regulatory notification.

Please initiate investigation immediately and advise on Field Alert Report requirements.
